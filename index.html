About the Author

Hi my name is Atlas! I am a (almost) four year old Great Dane English & Masstiff (also called a Daniff) dog who has just moved to Mission, BC from North Vancouver, BC. I live with my Mom and spare human, aka Dad. My hobbies include sleeping, crying for attention, and playing with my loud toys. I am a very friendly boy and love meeting new friends. I am also very curious and love exploring new places to pee on. I hope to share my adventures with you through this atlas!

My Favourite Places to Pee in my New Neighbourhood

Since moving to my new neighbourhood, I have discovered several favourite spots to pee on:

  1. The right back corner of the backyard. I deserve only the best private pee spots for me.
  2. The tree across the street from my house.
    • My Mom gets mad when I pee on the tree because it's in the neighbour's yard, but this one is fair game as its a tree and outside. Im not allowed to pee on anything inside so I'm following the rules.
  3. Hatzic Park!
    • it has so many new smells and other animals! My favourite friend is the giant black dog, mom says his name is holycrapabear, that is there sometimes but I'm not allowed to visit him despite he and mom are family!
  4. My Nonna and Papa's backyard!
    • I am so happy we moved closer to her but I will not go into the giant nature bath; I hate baths!
  5. The Christmas tree!
    • Mom and Dad get one every year for my gotcha day and decorate the house with lights and shiney snow ornaments. So to join in the celebration I try to pee on it for the whole month then they give me toys!

A Day in the Life of Atlas

I work hard to keep my humans entertained and on their toes. I have a very busy schedule that includes the following activities:

  • 1:42 am: claw my way under the blankets where mom and dad are sleeping and spin as many times as it takes to tangle myself up in all the blankets
  • 5am: dad leaves for work and I get my spot in the bed back
  • 8:45am: Mom leave for class and I get my spot in the bed back
  • 11:12am: Eat my breakfast then scream and cry until i get to go for a pee in the backyard even though mom is busy in class
  • 1:42pm: become captain chaos and play with the loudest toys i own while mom is on zoom
  • 6pm: Dad comes home and we play piggy in the middle; though pretty sure I am not a pig.
  • 8:52pm: It's bedtime but I am not tired so I try to get under the blankets again and start the cycle all over again
